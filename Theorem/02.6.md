
---
Lebesgue Outer Measure = 0
---

Let $E\in \mathfrak{P}(\mathbb{R})$. If $\mu^*_L(E)=0$ then $E\in \mathfrak{M}_L$.

\
\
\
\

---
Proof
---

For $A\in \mathfrak{P}(\mathbb{R})$

$$\begin{aligned}
\mu^*_L(A\cap E) + \mu^*_L(A\cap E^c)
&\leq \mu^*_L(E) + \mu^*_L(A) \\
&= \mu^*_L(A)
\end{aligned} $$

Thus we proved.
