```
Hahn-Banach Theorem for Real Linear Spaces
```

Let $(V, \mathbb{K}, ||\cdot||)$ and $(W, \mathbb{K}, ||\cdot||')$ be Banach spaces, let $L$ be a linear mapping of $V$ into $W$.

Then $L$ is an open mapping.
