```
Schwarz's Inequality
```

Let $(V, \langle \cdot,\cdot \rangle)$ be an inner product space,  and let $|| \cdot||$ be the function defined by
$
||v||:=\langle v,v \rangle
$ for $v\in V$.

Then

$$
|\langle v, w \rangle|
\leq
||v||_h ||w||_h
$$

for $v,w \in V$, and the equality holds if and only if $v$ and $w$ are linearly dependent.
