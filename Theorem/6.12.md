Let $(X, \mathfrak{A}, \mu)$ be a measure space, 
let $\{f_n: n \in \mathbb{N}\}$ be a sequence of extended real-valued $\mathfrak{A}$-measurable functions on a set $D$ such that $\mu(D)<\infty$.

If there exists a extended real-valued $\mathfrak{A}$-measurable function $f$ on $D$ such that
$
f_n \rightarrow f
$
a.e. on $D$,
then

$$
f_n \rightarrow f \ \ a.u. \ on \ D.
$$
