$$
||fg||_1\leq||f||_1 ||g||_\infty
$$

where the equality holds if and only if $0<||f||_1,||g||_\infty<\infty$ and 
$$
|g|=||g||_\infty \ \ a.e.\ on \ \{x\in X:f(x)\neq 0\}.
$$
