```
Borel-Cantelli Lemma
```
Let $(X, \mathfrak{A}, \mu)$ be a measure space,
and let $\{A_n: n \in \mathbb{N}\}$ be a sequence of measurable sets.

If 
$
\sum_{n \in \mathbb{N}} \mu(A_n) <\infty,
$ then

$$
\mu(\limsup_{n \rightarrow \infty} A_n)=0.
$$
